# Anxiety Checklist

Science-based anxiety & panic relief mobile app built to help people calm anxiety in the moment and retrain anxious thinking patterns long term.

## Features

- SOS Panic Button
- Guided Grounding Affirmations
- CBT Thought Journal
- Cognitive Distortion Detection
- Exposure Therapy Tools
- Belief Experiments
- Hypnosis & Meditation Sessions
- Sleep Audio Sessions
- Mood & Anxiety Tracking
- Walking Meditation

## About

Anxiety Checklist was created because anxiety and panic attacks rarely happen when people are “ready.”

Instead of overwhelming users with endless content, the app focuses on practical tools designed for real moments of anxiety, panic, overthinking, stress, and intrusive thoughts.
